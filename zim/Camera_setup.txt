====== Camera setup ======
Created zaterdag 24 oktober 2020

Tests of the setup: 
* 3D printing the tool holder
* 3D printing a easy way of photographing many tools in an easy way


Parts of the setup:
1. [[+Light]]
	a. 3-4 led lights/strips separately controllable to light from different angles
	b. with or without extra light on top
2. Wheel with tool mount
	a. 3D model creation of mount system
	b. creating wheel to be accurate
	c. controlling stepper motor to turn just enough to put the next tool in front of the camera
3. Camera mount
	a. Design to let the camera view different angles
	b. watch out for lighting
